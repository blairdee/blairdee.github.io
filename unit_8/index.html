Our Story

In 2017 I was inspired to create this caravan to bring a slice of Caribbean paradise to Baltimore City. With that goal in mind, I built this caribbean-on-the-go concept.

Using only the freshest ingredients, I draw on family recipes to craft authentic Bajan delicacies including rice and peas, macaroni pie, and Cou-Cou. Looking for something more compact? Try the beef patties or grilled fish tacos. Dig in!
