Courtney's Caribbean Caravan

Bite into paradise

In 2017 I was inspired to create this caravan to bring a slice of Caribbean paradise to Baltimore City. Using only the freshest ingredients, I draw on family recipes to craft authentic Bajan delicacies including rice and peas, macaroni pie, and Cou-Cou. Looking for something more compact? Try the beef patties or grilled fish tacos. Dig in!

Other menu items include:

  • Flying Fish
  • Cutters
  • Pudding and Souse
  • Conkies
